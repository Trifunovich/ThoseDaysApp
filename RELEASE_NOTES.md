### What's new

- **Period reminders** — turn on a gentle email a few days before your next expected period, from the new **Settings** page. Choose how many days ahead, and unsubscribe any time from a link in the email. Off by default, email only.
- **How sure we are** — predictions now show their confidence. When your cycles vary, the "next period" readout shows a likely window (e.g. "Mon–Thu") instead of a single date, and the Statistics page explains how regular your recent cycles have been.
- **Your data, yours to keep** — a new **Settings → Your data** section lets you download your history to a file and bring it back later. Imports are shown to you first and only change the dates the file covers — nothing is saved until you review it and click *Save this history permanently*, and your history outside those dates is never touched.
- **Settings page** — one place for your notification and data options.
