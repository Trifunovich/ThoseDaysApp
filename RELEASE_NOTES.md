### What's new

- **Today marker** — the current date is highlighted with an orange ring on the calendar
- **Countdown badge** — the next period's first day shows a countdown (e.g. "5d")
- **Next period readout** — the status bar now shows "Next period — in N days"
- **Return to today** — a "↩ Today" button appears when you've paged away from the current month
- **Live validation** — out-of-range warnings and future-date errors update as you type, no more surprise popups
- **Clearer prediction colors** — next predicted period in orange, future ones in gold, with a matching legend
- **Better dark theme** — improved contrast for predicted period markers on dark background
